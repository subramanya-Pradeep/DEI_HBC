Music and Dance

Folk Music

Bihar's folk music, including Bhojpuri, Maithili, and Magadhi songs, captures the essence of its rural life and cultural heritage.

Folk Dances

Traditional dances like Jat-Jatin and Bidesia are performed during festivals and special occasions, showcasing Bihar's rich performing arts.

Festivals of Bihar

Chhath Puja, Sama-Chakeva, and other vibrant festivals bring Bihar's culture to life.

Cuisine of Bihar

Litti-Chokha

This iconic dish is a must-try, made from roasted wheat balls (Litti) filled with sattu, served with a spicy vegetable mash (Chokha).

Sweets of Bihar

Delight in the sweetness of traditional Bihari sweets like Khaja and Anarsa, enjoyed during festivals and celebrations.
